Best Spots

Charyn Canyon

Often called the "Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan," this stunning natural wonder features towering red rock formations shaped by erosion over millions of years. It’s perfect for hiking, photography, and nature lovers.

Almaty and the Surrounding Mountains

The country's former capital offers a mix of urban and natural attractions, including the famous Medeu ice skating rink and Shymbulak ski resort. The nearby Big Almaty Lake is a must-visit for its striking turquoise waters.

Nur-Sultan (Astana)

The modern capital of Kazakhstan is known for its futuristic architecture, including the Baiterek Tower, Khan Shatyr, and the Palace of Peace and Reconciliation. It’s a hub for culture, history, and entertainment.

Kaindy Lake

This surreal underwater forest, formed after an earthquake, features submerged spruce trees rising from the water. The lake's striking blue-green hue makes it a unique destination for adventure seekers and photographers.

Baikonur Cosmodrome

The world’s oldest and largest space launch facility, Baikonur offers a chance to witness real rocket launches. Space enthusiasts can visit the Gagarin’s Start launch pad, where Yuri Gagarin took off for the first human spaceflight.
