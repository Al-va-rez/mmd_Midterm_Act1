Welcome to Kazakhstan

Location

Kazakhstan, the largest landlocked country, is located in Central Asia and partly in Eastern Europe, bordering Russia, China, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan. Its strategic position connects it to both Asian and European markets.

Significance

Historically, Kazakhstan was a key part of the Silk Road and is now home to the Baikonur Cosmodrome, the worldâ€™s first and largest space launch facility. It is also a major global producer of oil, gas, and uranium, playing a crucial role in energy markets.

Uniqueness

Culturally, Kazakhstan blends nomadic traditions, Turkic roots, and Russian influence. Its landscapes range from the Altai Mountains to vast steppes and deserts. The capital, Astana (formerly Nur-Sultan), stands out for its futuristic architecture and rapid development.
